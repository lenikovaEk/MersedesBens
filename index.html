The VISION AVTR

Inspered by the future

CONCEPT

Electromobility in harmony with man and nature. Eco-friendly organic battery technology and sophisticated operational strategy for the VISION AVTR concept car.

premier

A new concept Mercedes-Benz, the VISION AVTR, was unveiled at CES 2020. The futuristic concept of the presented electric vehicle unites man, nature and the car in a unique way.

Ola Kallenius, Chairman of the Board of Directors of Daimler AG and Mercedes-Benz AG, presented the concept car in a speech along with Academy Award winner and Avatar filmmaker James Cameron.

It was the philosophy created by the cult director in a fantasy film that became the basis for the creation of VISION AVTR.

“Mercedes-Benz has always been one of the most technologically advanced premium brands. Now it's time to combine luxury and sustainability. After all, only in this way can we meet the standards of the future.”

INTERIOR & EXTERIOR

Design VISION AVTR - the epitome of sustainability

The groundbreaking VISION AVTR concept combines the sustainable interplay of interior, exterior and UX design. The entire design process was focused on a specific result - a unique experience of interaction and perception of the concept car by the driver and passengers. It was about creating a unique space in which passengers have a biometric connection with each other, with the vehicle and the outside world.

The outer side opening extends through the interior and creates an endless loop inspired by the sacred connection between the Na'vi people in the movie Avatar and their natural habitat.

Also, with its elongated "One Bow" design and organic design philosophy, the VISION AVTR represents a radical new stage in the automotive industry. Thus, having gone from the concept of awareness to the unique fusion of man and car, Mercedes-Benz engineers succeeded in creating the VISION AVTR concept electric vehicle.

A concise continuation of the external minimalist design are the front seats, made in a very organic form that resembles leafy hammocks from the planet Pandora. The center console symbolizes the Tree of Souls, the Na'vi's most sacred site. The control unit is intuitive and incredibly functional.

iIn addition, the control center is located in such a way that it is easily accessible to both the driver and passenger, as well as rear passengers.

In this way, VISION AVTR establishes a close biometric connection with the driver and helps to increase awareness of the surrounding situation.
